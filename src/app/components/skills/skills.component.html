Soft skills

As a developer your ability to code is paramount, however, I believe having soft skills can over rank tech skills in some areas. I have met a few 'Rockstars' in my career and while they can churn out impressive quantities of code, their ability to work as part of a larger team actually makes them 'negative coders'. However great they are, generally a single person cannot do everything.

I like to think that I am very easy going and down to earth. I enjoy taking the initiative, steering or being part of a team, knowledge sharing, coaching and being challenged on design and technical decisions. I've had enough bad jobs to know that development is a great career, and however stressful this may get, it pales in comparison to some of the factories I have worked in :)
